Rules

  1. You must follow the Bidding Instructions – a separate document – to place bids.
  2. By placing a bid, you have contractually obligated yourself to purchase the artwork at that price, unless a higher, valid bid is placed.
  3. Art Show staff must be immediately notified for any “Buy Now” bid, mistaken bids, or requests to “up” your own bid; do not make these modifications yourself.
  4. All “won” artwork must be paid-for and collected during sales periods on Sunday or Monday. Otherwise, you will be charged for the packaging and shipping costs to deliver the purchases to you.
  5. By California law we, as a house of consignment, are obligated to provide the artists of pieces you purchased a statement detailing your name and contact information.
  6. No photography or videotaping. Cameras will be confiscated.
  7. Failure to abide by these rules or the Bidding Instructions may result in cancelled bids, ejection from the art show, or cancellation of convention membership.
